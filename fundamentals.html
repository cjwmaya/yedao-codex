14 Fundamentals
基础原理速查

14 个原理 · 三层结构

A complete cheat sheet of the 14 fundamentals. Each principle includes the English name, Chinese translation, the tier it belongs to, and a one-line summary. Use this as a daily reference while building.
14 个基础原理的完整速查表。每个原理含英文名、中文翻译、所属层级、一句话总结。可作为日常构建时的参考清单。

Tier One · Foundation
第一层 · 基础（生成前）

Foundation is the work you do before you generate a single image. It answers three questions: what does this world believe, what happened before we arrived, and what figures carry its meaning.
基础层是你生成第一张图之前要做的事。它回答三个问题：这个世界相信什么？我们到来之前发生了什么？哪些形象承载它的意义？

Tier Two · Craft
第二层 · 技艺（让世界可见）

Craft is where the world becomes visible. It governs how your world looks and feels across every single output. These are not random choices — they are a system.
技艺层是世界变得可见的地方。它决定你的世界在每一张图上的样子与感觉。这些不是随机选择——它们是一个系统。

Tier Three · Direction
第三层 · 方向（组织成世界）

Direction is the hardest discipline because it asks you to stop generating and start thinking. It is the art of sequencing, selecting, and knowing when something is done.
方向是最难的纪律——它要求你停下来，别再生成，开始思考。它是排序、选择、知道何时收手的艺术。

"These are not rules. They are lenses — ways of looking at your work and asking whether it holds together, whether it earns its place, whether it breathes."
"这些不是规则。它们是镜片——让你重新审视自己的作品：它是否兜得住、它是否配得上它的位置、它是否在呼吸。"
