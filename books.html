Art & Design

艺术与设计书籍 · 影响法典思考的书

A curated shelf of art and design books that informed the Codex. These are the references behind the references.
一份精心挑选的艺术与设计书单，构成了法典背后的视觉养分。

01

The Story of Art · 艺术的故事

— E.H. Gombrich

The classic survey of art history. Best read cover-to-cover, then kept on the shelf.
艺术史的经典概览。最适合通读一遍，再放在书架上随时翻。

02

Cinematography · 电影摄影术

— Kris Malkiewicz

The book that taught me to see frames. Mandatory if Cinematique interests you.
教我用"帧"的眼睛看世界的书。

03

The Shape of Design · 设计的形体

— Frank Chimero

On craft as a way of thinking. Short, generous, and lasting.
把技艺视为思考方式。短小、温厚、读后难忘。

04

The Architecture of Happiness · 幸福的建筑

— Alain de Botton

Why buildings feel the way they feel, and what that means for the worlds you build.
为什么建筑有那种"感觉"，以及这对你构建的世界意味着什么。

05

Color and Light · 颜色与光

— James Gurney

A practical guide to painting light, useful for anyone composing with AI.
关于"画光线"的实用指南，对用 AI 做构图的人同样受用。

06

Ways of Seeing · 观看之道

— John Berger

How we learn to see — and how the world teaches us what to see. A foundational text on visual literacy.
我们如何学会看——以及世界如何教我们去看。关于视觉素养的奠基之作。
