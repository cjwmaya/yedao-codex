What is 野岛社区
野岛社区是什么

野岛社区 is a Chinese-language replica of the VVSVS World Building Codex Library by Ivan Flugelman. The goal is to make the Codex's 14 fundamentals of world building — Thesis, Lore, Archetypes, Visual Language, Composition, Tone, Atmosphere, Scale, Story, Pacing, Editing, Repetition & Variation, Signature — accessible to a Chinese-speaking audience.
野岛社区是 VVSVS（《世界构建法典》）作者 Ivan Flugelman 作品的中文复刻版。

Original Source
原作出处

The original VVSVS World Building Codex is created by Ivan Flugelman, a creative director and independent researcher.
原版《世界构建法典》由Ivan Flugelman 创作，他是一名创意总监兼独立研究者。

About This Replica
关于本复刻

This replica is built for educational purposes — to study and discuss the theory. It mirrors the structure, the content, and the design language of the original Codex site, with bilingual annotations.
本复刻为学习与讨论理论而建。它还原了原版法典站点的结构、内容与设计语言。
