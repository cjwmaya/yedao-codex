World Building
Codex 3.0

世界构建法典 · 3.0

14 fundamentals of building consistent fictional worlds. Free framework for the AI era. By Ivan Flugelman.
14 个构建一致虚构世界的基础原理。AI 时代的免费框架。作者：Ivan Flugelman。

The principles that exist underneath the tools, the trends, and the aesthetics — the ones that do not change. Codex 3.0 strips everything back to the 14 core fundamentals, organized into Foundation, Craft, and Direction.
那些隐藏在工具、趋势与美学之下、不随时间改变的原则。法典 3.0 把一切剥到最底层，凝练为 14 个核心原理。

Why a Codex
为什么需要法典

Tools change. Models evolve. Features disappear overnight. If you build your creative practice on top of features, you're building on sand.
工具在变，模型在迭代，特性一夜消失。如果把创作根基搭在"特性"之上，那就等于建在沙上。

"Most people generate images. A few build worlds. The difference is not talent or taste or even time. It is structure."
"多数人在生成图像，少数人在构建世界。差异不在天赋、品味、甚至时间，而在结构。"

Tier One — Foundation
第一层 · 基础（生成前）

Foundation is the work you do before you generate a single image. It answers three questions: what does this world believe, what happened before we arrived, and what figures carry its meaning.
基础层是你生成第一张图之前要做的事。它回答三个问题。

Tier Two — Craft
第二层 · 技艺（让世界可见）

Craft is where the world becomes visible. It governs how your world looks and feels across every single output. These are not random choices — they are a system.
技艺层是世界变得可见的地方。

Tier Three — Direction
第三层 · 方向（组织成世界）

Direction is the hardest discipline because it asks you to stop generating and start thinking.
方向是最难的纪律——它要求你停下来，别再生成，开始思考。

"These are not rules. They are lenses — ways of looking at your work and asking whether it holds together, whether it earns its place, whether it breathes."
"这些不是规则。它们是镜片——让你重新审视自己的作品。"

The Ten Worlds of V3
第三卷的十个世界

The codex doesn't just teach principles in the abstract. It applies them across ten distinct style archetypes — ten fully realized worlds.
法典不仅在抽象层面讲原理。它把原理落到十个差异巨大的风格原型上。

What This Codex Is For
这本法典的用途

The World Building Codex v3 is not a tutorial. It is a framework for thinking about worlds — a structure you can return to long after the tools have changed.
世界构建法典 v3 不是教程。它是用来思考世界的框架——一种结构，在工具迭代多年之后仍可反复回归。
